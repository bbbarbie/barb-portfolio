BeatMatch

AI Dance Coach (Because I Teach Dance But Can't Count Beats)

6+ years as a dance instructor. Still can't count beats. I feel the music instead, which is a problem when students need "5, 6, 7, 8." So I built the coach I needed: it hears the beat (Web Audio BPM detection), watches the body (33 keypoints via MoveNet/MediaPipe), scores every move against a ±150 ms beat window, and has Claude write the coaching report. It listens and watches.
